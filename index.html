Welcome

W.T. Boone Manufacturing is a full-service manufacturing facility specializing in accurate, cost-effective Computer Numeric Control (CNC) machining solutions. We combine the highest quality equipment, tooling, and gauges with an experienced staff to manufacture custom components for our clients. Whether a single, special-order piece, or a large bulk order, we have a service that can meet your needs.

Our Mission Statement:

  • To manufacture high-quality components — on-time, the first time
  • To use only the highest quality machinery, tools, and gauges
  • To treat customers, vendors, and employees like family
  • To support the local business community
