Equipment

Okuma Captain L-370

The Okuma Captain L-370 is a versatile 2-axis turning machine. It has a twelve station turret with a maximum X-axis travel of 20.8” and Z-axis travel of 20.47”. The Captain is capable of turning pieces up to 14.56” in diameter and at rates up to 3,500 RPM. Both of our Captain L-370s are outfitted with LNS bar feeders with the capacity to feed material up to 2” diameter.

Okuma LB-9

The Okuma LB-9 is our second operation machine. It has an eight station turret. The Okuma LB-9 can accommodate pieces up to 10 inches in diameter and at rates up to 7,000 RPM.

Daewoo 3016L

The Daewoo 3016L is our newest vertical milling machine. It has a 12,000 RPM 20-hp spindle motor and 30 tool magazine. The 3016L also has a maximum X-axis travel of 30 in., Y axis travel of 16.1 in., and Z-axis of 20.1 in at rapid travel rates up to 1,417 IPM.

Amada HA 250W

The Amada HA 250W is an automatic feed horizontal band saw with the capacity to cut round material ranging from .3-10 inches in diameter. Our Amada saw is also equipped with a 13’ roller table.

Okuma 4020

The Okuma 4020 has an 8,000 RPM 15-hp spindle motor and 15 tool magazine. It has a maximum X-axis travel of 40 in., a maximum Y-axis travel of 20 in, and rigid tapping ability.

OKUMA MC 4-VA

The Okuma 4-VA has an 6,000 RPM 10-hp spindle. X axis travel of 32 inches and Y axis travel of 16 inches
